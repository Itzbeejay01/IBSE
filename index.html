What is IBSE 1.0?

IBSE 1.0 is a worship metting birthed as an instruction from God to raise an atmosphere of intimacy where Jesus is the central focus. It is a call to stir hearts, unify believers, and create a space where heaven invades the earth through intense worship, the Word, and spiritual alignment.Through music, prayer, and deep encounters, IBSE is more than an event it's a movement of revival in Ibadan.
